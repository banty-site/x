FAQs
Is Banty a drug wholesale shop ?

No. We connect pharmacies and OTCMS to wholesalers. That's it.


Have you begun operations?

We will officially begin on September 9th.


What if I don't trust you?

We can understand. We will deliver the drugs to you before you make payment in that case. Or simply send the the wholesale shop your cash via wire.


What are your working hours?

We are online 24/7. You can order anytime but of course, we can only get your items when the shop you are buying from is open.


What if my order is missing some item?

We would contact the wholesaler you bought from and correct the mistake as soon as possible at no additional cost.
